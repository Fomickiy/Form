Calm rest

The policy covers medical and travel expenses. As well as the costs of treating injuries received during these classes:

  • Fitness, yoga, Pilates
  • Aerobics, water aerobics
  • Golf
  • Skiing other than alpine skiing

  • Fishing (except underwater fishing)

Professional sports

Includes Quiet Recreation, Active Recreation and Dangerous Sports.
